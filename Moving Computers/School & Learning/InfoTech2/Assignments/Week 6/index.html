Instructions

To begin, click "Earn Money" to start earning money. Your wages at the beginning are fairly low. To increase them, you need to get paid more at gigs, which requires more skill. To increase your skill,take some guitar lessons by clicking "Take Guitar Lesson". Once you reach the 5th skill level, your pay will increase and you will enter Stage 1! Your pay will continue to increase until you hit level 20, where you are skilled enough to continue your rockstar journey without anymore lessons!

When you reach Stage 2 (all stage requirements can be found at the bottom of instructions), you can start earning even more by buying ads, hiring a marketing team, etc.

  • Stage 1: Reach level 5
  • Stage 2: Reach level 20, buy new guitar, hire a drummer, vocalist, and bassist
